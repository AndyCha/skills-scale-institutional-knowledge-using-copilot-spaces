# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers research user needs, create intuitive interfaces, and ensure the product delivers an excellent user experience. They collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to translate user requirements into visual and interaction designs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Define information architecture and user flows
- Develop and maintain design systems and style guides
- Collaborate with developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Iterate on designs based on user feedback and analytics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Reduce user friction and support costs through better design
- Advocate for user needs throughout the development process

### Typical Communication
- Weekly design reviews with Product and Engineering
- User research findings presentations
- Design critiques and collaborative workshops
- Handoff documentation and design specs for developers

---

## QA Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality through systematic testing, automation, and process improvement. They work closely with Developers and Product Managers to verify that features meet requirements and maintain quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and execute test plans and test cases
- Create and maintain automated test suites
- Perform functional, regression, and integration testing
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Contribute to continuous improvement of QA processes

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Increase test coverage and automation
- Reduce manual testing effort through automation
- Improve overall product quality and reliability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Test reports and quality metrics
- Bug triage meetings
- QA signoff on releases

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create clear, comprehensive documentation for internal and external audiences. They collaborate with Product and Engineering teams to document features, APIs, processes, and best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user-facing documentation
- Create internal process documentation and runbooks
- Document APIs, SDKs, and integration guides
- Develop tutorials, guides, and troubleshooting content
- Review technical content for accuracy and clarity
- Manage documentation versioning and publishing

### Goals
- Make product features easily discoverable and understandable
- Reduce support burden through comprehensive documentation
- Ensure documentation stays current with product changes
- Improve user and developer onboarding experience

### Typical Communication
- Weekly syncs with Product and Engineering
- Documentation reviews with subject matter experts
- Content planning and information architecture discussions
- User feedback and documentation metrics reviews

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure the security posture of projects through threat modeling, security reviews, and compliance oversight. They work across teams to identify risks, implement controls, and respond to security incidents.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Define security requirements and controls
- Oversee vulnerability management and remediation
- Lead security incident response and investigations
- Ensure compliance with security policies and standards
- Provide security training and awareness

### Goals
- Protect customer data and organizational assets
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and attack surface
- Ensure compliance with regulatory and policy requirements
- Build a culture of security awareness

### Typical Communication
- Security review meetings during planning
- Threat model workshops
- Incident response coordination
- Security advisories and remediation guidance

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and operational tooling. They enable reliable, automated delivery of software and ensure system reliability and observability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting
- Automate deployment and operational tasks
- Support incident response and troubleshooting
- Optimize system performance and reliability

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable software delivery
- Minimize deployment risks and downtime
- Improve system observability and operational efficiency
- Reduce manual operational overhead through automation

### Typical Communication
- Deployment planning and release coordination
- Incident retrospectives and postmortems
- Infrastructure and architecture reviews
- Operational metrics and SLA reporting

---

## Customer Support Representative

### Role Summary
Customer Support Representatives serve as the voice of the customer, providing frontline support and gathering feedback. They collaborate with Product and Engineering teams to resolve issues and improve the customer experience.

### Responsibilities
- Respond to customer inquiries and issues
- Troubleshoot problems and provide solutions
- Escalate complex issues to appropriate teams
- Document common issues and solutions in knowledge bases
- Gather and communicate customer feedback
- Contribute to support process improvements

### Goals
- Deliver excellent customer service and satisfaction
- Resolve issues quickly and effectively
- Reduce customer effort and frustration
- Advocate for customer needs in product development

### Typical Communication
- Daily ticket triage and escalations
- Weekly feedback sessions with Product team
- Support metrics and trend reporting
- Knowledge base and runbook updates
